What is Valorant?

Valorant is a free to play first person shooting game, created by Riot Games. There are unique agents with specific roles/play styles that players can choose.

  • Duelist

    Duelist agents are self-sufficient fraggers who their team expects, through abilities and skills, to get high frags and seek out engagements first.

  • Controller

    Controller agents are experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success.

  • Initiator

    Initiator agents challenge angles by setting up their team to enter contested ground and push defenders away.

  • Sentinel

    Sentinel agents are defensive experts who can lock down areas and watch flanks, both on attacker and defender rounds.

What Agent should you play?

If you are...

Aggresive

I recommend playing Duelist as they have can enter a site and potentially get a pick or two with your team following up!

Defensive

I recommend playing Sentinel as they have utility to either lock down a site or watch for sneaky flanks!

Strategic

I recommend playing Controller as they can smoke of sites creating an easier way to push or use it as fake!

Informative

I recommend playing Initiator as they can use abilities to gather intel on who's on site and to clear certain areas!
